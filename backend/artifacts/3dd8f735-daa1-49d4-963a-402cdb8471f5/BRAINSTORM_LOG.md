### Problem Framing

- Autonomy at scale creates complex, dynamic problems
- Existing protocols are either too centralized or too fragmented for optimal team performance
- Need a hybrid system: flexible, secure, and adaptive

### Wild Ideas

- **AI-driven Task Prioritizer**: uses machine learning to predict impact and allocate resources accordingly
- **Bi-directional Communication Hub**: creates a seamless, two-way connection between the team and the protocol
- **Dynamic Resource Allocation**: reassigns personnel in real-time based on shifting project priorities

### System Shape

- **System Components:**
  - **AI Engine**: central decision-making core
  - **Communication Hub**: interfaces between team members, task managers, and the AI Engine
  - **Resource Manager**: allocates personnel, tools, and resources
  - **Feedback Loop**: continuously captures and refines team behavior
- **Interconnected System Diagram**: illustrates relationships and workflow between components

### Feasibility Notes

- **AI Engine:** requires significant computing power and data storage
- **Communication Hub:** must be user-friendly, secure, and robust
- **Resource Manager:** demands real-time scalability and adaptability
- **Feedback Loop:** needs regular, thorough evaluations to maintain system efficacy

### Key Decisions

- **Team composition:** minimum of 3 experts in AI engineering, system design, and project management
- **Project Timeline:** 6 weeks from initiation to prototype development
- **Resource Allocation:** prioritize AI Engine development, followed by Communication Hub and Resource Manager integration
- **Scope Management:** maintain a flexible scope, allowing for iterative adjustments and refinements

### Action Items

- Complete AI Engine prototype within the first 2 weeks
- Develop Communication Hub and Resource Manager concurrently
- Establish Feedback Loop and conduct regular reviews

### Risks

- Over-reliance on AI Engine could hinder human decision-making
- System failure or data loss could compromise team productivity and reputation
- Resource constraints may impact timelines or team morale
