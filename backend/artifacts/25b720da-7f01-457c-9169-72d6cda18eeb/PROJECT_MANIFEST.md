### PROJECT_MANIFEST.md Artifact

#### Overview
------

The Autonomous Office Protocol is a calculator system that will provide users with a reliable and user-friendly tool for calculations.


#### Scope
------

The scope of the project includes:

*   Developing a calculator system that meets user requirements
*   Implementing accurate calculations for integer and non-integer division
*   Handling overflow for large numbers
*   Providing a user-friendly interface


#### Out of Scope
------

The following are out of scope:

*   Developing advanced mathematical functions beyond basic arithmetic operations
*   Creating a graphical user interface (GUI)


#### Risks
------

*   Risk: The calculator may not meet user requirements.
	+ Mitigation: Regular user testing and feedback will be used to ensure that the calculator meets user requirements.
*   Risk: The calculator may have bugs or errors.
	+ Mitigation: Thorough testing and debugging will be used to identify and fix any bugs or errors.
*   Risk: The calculator may not be user-friendly.
	+ Mitigation: User testing and feedback will be used to ensure that the calculator is user-friendly and easy to use.
*   Risk: The calculator may not handle non-integer division correctly.
	+ Mitigation: Clearly document the calculator's behavior for non-integer division and consider adding a warning or error message to handle this scenario.
*   Risk: The calculator may overflow for large numbers.
	+ Mitigation: Research and document how the calculator will handle large numbers and consider implementing overflow handling mechanisms, such as saturating arithmetic or throwing an exception.
*   Risk: The calculator may not be compatible with all devices or browsers.
	+ Mitigation: Thorough testing on various devices and browsers will be conducted to ensure compatibility.
